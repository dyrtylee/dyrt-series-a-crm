## Elevator Pitch (60 seconds)

> We didn't design a waste app. We collected waste until we understood it.
>
> In 2024, we built a composting facility in Vernon, CA — no software, just permits, trucks, and 990,000 lbs/month of food waste from enterprise customers. To manage it, we built internal tracking tools. Disney, Marriott, AEG, and Ritz-Carlton found the dashboards and signed up.
>
> That's when we realized: the facility paid for the software. The software unlocked the enterprise.
>
> Today, Dyrt is the physical and digital backbone of waste management — AI bill ingestion, real-time diversion dashboards, EPA-verified Scope 3 reporting, and owned composting infrastructure that routes organic waste out of landfills. $1M ARR, 450% YoY, 0% churn. 45 enterprise customers. 525 Sprouts locations contracted. $21M pipeline we can't serve fast enough.
>
> Most platforms stop at data. We built the infrastructure too. That's the moat — permitted facilities take 6+ months to obtain and can't be replicated quickly.
>
> We're raising $20M Series A to close the gap between the demand we have and the capacity we're building.
