# Intro Request — Ask for Warm Introduction

**Use for**: Asking a relationship to introduce you to a target investor
**Tone**: Concise, make it easy for the introducer, forwardable blurb included

---

## Template A — To Board Member / Existing Investor

**Subject**: Intro request — [Target Firm]

Hey [First],

Could you intro me to [Name] at [Firm]? They're [one line on why they fit — e.g., "running the composting fund at Closed Loop, perfect thesis match"].

I'd use the blurb below if you want to just forward it:

---

*Forwarding on behalf of Lee Robinson, CEO of Dyrt Labs — they're the ERP of Modern Waste. $1M ARR, 450% YoY, 0% churn. Customers include Disney, Marriott, Intuit Dome, AEG, Sprouts. Raising $20M Series A. The [waste / circular / climate] thesis maps directly. Lee is great — worth a quick call.*

*Lee Robinson | lee@dyrt.co | 813.390.8559*

---

Thanks,

Lee
