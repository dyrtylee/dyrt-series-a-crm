## Urgency Creation Tactics

1. **Artificial deadline**: "We're targeting a close by July 15. If you want to be included, I need to know your intent by [date]."
2. **Social proof**: "We have [X] committed / in diligence." (Share when true.)
3. **FOMO close**: "Noro-Moseley / [other firm] is moving quickly — we expect the lead to be locked in the next 2 weeks."
4. **NM deadline**: "The NM LEDA incentives have a commitment deadline tied to facility groundbreak."
5. **Customer momentum**: "We just signed [customer] and have [X] locations going live. This is the moment."
