## Why NM Dedicated Fund = Dyrt Fit
Lowercarbon's NM fund has a mandate to invest in companies with NM operations. Dyrt's composting facility in NM is a $6.5M build with $12M in confirmed state incentives — this is exactly the kind of NM-anchored deal their fund is designed for.

Carbon angle: Every ton of food waste composted instead of landfilled avoids ~0.6 metric tons of CO2e (methane). At 100 tons/day capacity (36,500 tons/year), that's **~21,900 tons CO2e avoided annually per facility**. Scale to 6 facilities = 131,000+ tons CO2e/year.
